About Montville HOA

Our Community

Montville Place Sauyo's Homeowners Association is a vibrant community of over 500 homes committed to maintaining a safe, beautiful, and harmonious neighborhood.

Established in 2005, our HOA has consistently worked to enhance property values and quality of life through community programs, security initiatives, and maintenance of common areas.

Community Features:

  • 24/7 Security with CCTV Monitoring
  • Landscaped Parks and Play Areas
  • Swimming Pool and Clubhouse
  • Vehicle Access Control System

Vehicle Management System

Our Vehicle Entry-Exit System provides residents with secure and convenient access to the community while maintaining strict security protocols.

Resident Vehicles

Visitor Access

Pre-register visitors through the portal or mobile app for seamless entry.

Delivery Services
